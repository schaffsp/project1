Plant Based Options

Plant based meat alternatives come in many different forms, but all generally try to emulate the taste, texture, and smell of real meat. Plant based ground, or crumbles, are very common in grocery stores, and there are now alternatives for all sorts of things like bacon, grilled chicken, lunch meat, and more. Most plant based meats are made from soy or pea protein.
